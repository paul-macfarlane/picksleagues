## Summary

Adds static `/privacy` and `/terms` pages under the `(public)` route group, plus
a shared `<SiteFooter>` that links to both. Footer is mounted in
`(public)/layout.tsx` and in `AppShell` (which covers `(app)` and `(setup)`),
so every page gets the links.

## Business Spec Coverage

N/A — BUSINESS_SPEC does not cover legal pages. Content is authored to match
actual app behavior: OAuth sign-in (Google/Discord), soft-anonymization on
delete (§2.3), no ads/monetization, personal project provided as-is. Contact is
`picksleagues@gmail.com`.

## Decisions

- **Static in-page content, no CMS.** Matches the story and keeps the surface
  trivial; if legal copy grows we can revisit MDX later.
- **Last-updated date hard-coded** (`April 13, 2026`). A dynamic date would lie
  about when the policy actually changed. Update the constant when the copy
  changes.
- **Moved vertical centering out of `(public)/layout.tsx`.** The layout
  previously used `justify-center` to center the login card, which would push
  long content pages (privacy/terms, and later PL-053 landing) toward the
  middle of the viewport. The layout now renders main as a plain flex column;
  the login page centers itself in its own wrapper. Keeps each page in control
  of its own vertical rhythm.
- **Shared footer component.** Reused across `(public)` and `AppShell` rather
  than duplicating markup — a single place to update links and copyright.
- **No copyright line in the footer.** There is no registered copyright to
  assert; dropped the `© YEAR PicksLeagues` line and left just the
  Privacy/Terms nav.
- **Legal pages live in their own `(legal)` route group with an auth-aware
  layout.** Privacy and Terms are reachable whether signed in or not, so the
  layout checks the session: authed users with a profile get the full
  `AppShell` (user menu, theme picker in menu); everyone else gets a minimal
  public-style header with `ThemeToggle`. `UnauthorizedError` from
  `getSession()` is treated as "not signed in" rather than rethrown, because
  these pages must stay accessible without auth. Other errors still propagate.
- **Contact is `picksleagues@gmail.com`** as a `mailto:` link on both pages.

## Deviations from the Story

- None of substance. The story says "Footer links from (public) and (app)
  layouts" — `(app)` layout uses `AppShell`, so mounting the footer in
  `AppShell` covers it (and also covers `(setup)`, which shares the shell).

## Notable / Gotchas

- **Login page now centers itself.** If you change `(public)/layout.tsx` back
  to `justify-center`, remove the wrapper in `login/page.tsx` or the card
  will be double-centered.
- **Tailwind `prose` classes** are used on the article wrappers but the project
  does not install `@tailwindcss/typography`. Left them in as a no-op marker
  — harmless today, and if typography is added later the pages pick it up for
  free. Remove if it causes confusion.
- **Footer lives below `main`**, not inside it. `main` keeps `flex-1` so the
  footer sticks to the bottom on short pages.
